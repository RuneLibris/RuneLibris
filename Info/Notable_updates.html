Notable updates[edit]

Date Update details Ref
29 March 2004 Release of RuneScape 2 [2]
31 May 2005 Introduction of the instance system [3]
16 May 2006 Maps become encrypted (XTEAs start being used) [4]
01 July 2008 Release of RuneScape HD [5]
22 November 2011 Release of the "2012 style" interface [6]
20 November 2012 Release of Evolution of Combat [7]
22 July 2013 Release of RuneScape 3 [8]
14 July 2014 Release of Legacy Mode [9]
18 April 2016 Release of the NXT client [10]
17 June 2021 Release of the Mobile client [11]
