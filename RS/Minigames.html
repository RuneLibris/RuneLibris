Overview[edit]

This page lists the update history of Minigames in RuneScape.

Archive details[edit]

  1. Minigame release date data was extracted from RuneScape Wiki using a DPL and then converted to RuneLibris format using LibreOffice and Notepad++[1].
  2. Using another RuneScape Wiki DPL, a list of removed minigames was gathered and then added as removal entries on this page[2].
