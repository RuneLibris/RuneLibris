Overview[edit]

Wolf Whistle is a quest in RuneScape. It is notable for being pratically lost media; the quest was replaced by a newer variant on 31 January 2012[1] so it does not exist in RuneScape 3. The quest also does not exists in Old School RuneScape either. It was also removed prior to May 2012; meaning it was never included in Jagex's backups[2]
