Discontinuation Notice

As you may have noticed, there was not any activity on RuneLibris since 14 June 2025. This because I lost interest in this project. RuneScape is a game too complex to archive. I had tried to account for this by limiting the scope of the project, but that didn't work.

As such, I have decided to move on from any RuneScape Preservation activities. I will focus my attention on other projects I can actually finish.

The final version of RuneLibris will be uploaded on this Internet Archive page (the final version will have the filename "RuneLibris (2025).vhd"). The file will be uploaded at some point before 31 December 2025.

For any future inquiry, I refer you to my personal blog for more information.

Written by manpaint on 10 December 2025.
